Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about selling on our platform

We believe in supporting artisans, which is why it's completely free to join and list your products. We only take a small commission (15%) on each sale to help cover payment processing and platform maintenance.

We process payments through secure payment gateways. You'll receive payments directly to your bank account on a bi-weekly basis. We provide detailed sales reports and you can track your earnings in your seller dashboard.

Currently, we focus on physical handmade products. We don't support digital products or services at this time. All items must be handmade or hand-altered by you or your team.

We provide comprehensive support including marketing tools, seller resources, and a dedicated support team. You'll have access to analytics, promotional opportunities, and a community of fellow artisans to help you grow your business.
