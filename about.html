Our Vision

Empowering artisans, preserving traditions, and building sustainable futures

La Vie Local's Story

Founded in 2025, La Vie Local is a small initiative to connect local artisans with conscious consumers who value craftsmanship and sustainability of local vendors.

What started as a niche project has grown into a widely known marketplace that supports local artisans from Mauritius. We believe that every handmade item tells a story, and every purchase makes a difference in someone's life.

Our journey has been guided by a simple but powerful idea: that ethical business can be a force for good in the world, creating opportunities for talented people while preserving traditional and original crafts.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do

Empowerment

We empower artisans by providing fair wages, skill development, and access to markets, helping them build sustainable livelihoods.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from sustainable sourcing to minimal packaging, ensuring our planet thrives for generations to come.

Community

We believe in the power of community to create positive change, connecting makers and buyers in meaningful ways.
