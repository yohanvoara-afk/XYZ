The Power of Passed Pawns

Passed pawns are among the most powerful weapons in chess. These pawns cannot be stopped by enemy pawns and can potentially promote to queens.

Golden Rule: "Passed pawns must be pushed!"

Types of Passed Pawns

Protected Passed Pawn

Defended by another pawn, cannot be captured by pieces.

Example: White pawn on d5 protected by e4 pawn

Outside Passed Pawn

Far from main action, creates decoy threats.

Example: Passed pawn on a-file while action in center

How to Use Passed Pawns

Key Principles:

  • Advance with piece support
  • Create distractions elsewhere
  • Use as decoy for bigger gains
  • Promote to winning material
Remember: Unsupported passed pawns are weak!
